Privacy Policy for Ristal Bridge Academy

Effective Date: 05/10/2024

At Ristal Bridge Academy, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Demographics: We collect age, grade level, and location to personalize content and marketing.
  • Usage Patterns: We track app usage frequency, duration, and engagement with specific features and content.
  • Learning Analytics: We gather assessment scores, progress tracking, and course completion rates to enhance learning outcomes.
  • Feedback: We collect survey responses and open-ended feedback to improve our services.
  • Interaction Data: We monitor social interactions and collaboration metrics to assess community engagement.
  • Content Engagement: We analyze time spent on lessons to understand user interest.
  • Retention Data: We track user retention and reasons for discontinuation to enhance the user experience.
  • Device Data: We gather information on the devices used for optimization purposes.
  • Referral Sources: We collect data on how users found us to refine marketing strategies.
  • Gamification Metrics: We analyze engagement with gamified elements to assess their impact.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Personalize user experience and content delivery.
  • Enhance learning outcomes through tailored resources.
  • Improve app features and functionality.
  • Conduct marketing efforts and communicate updates.

3. Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to comply with legal processes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of certain data collection practices by adjusting your app settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

Ristal Bridge Academy is intended for secondary school students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent.
